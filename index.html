How to Buy Jeans Online

A tranquil guide to calmly replacing your favorite pants, in 10 stages.

Peruse the latest catwalk fashions and decide on a style and color. Search through social networks such as Polyvore and Lyst for similar styles that are in your budget. Visit the relevant retailers' websites, and proceed to measure yourself a minimum of 12 times in composed, yet failed attempts to fit yourself within a pre-ordained sizing chart.

Curse serenely at aforementioned charts.
Order 12 different pairs of jeans from 12 different retailers, in 12 different measurements.
Spend one week in meditation waiting for your selections to arrive.
 Try on the jeans, one by one. Try on the last, final pair, in a sedately Hail Mary attempt to find a decent pair of jeans.
Renew your commitment to pajamas.
 Repeat graciously every 18 months.
